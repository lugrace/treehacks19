ABOUT SUST

Itâ€™s easy to understand how taking shorter showers saves water, or how biking instead of driving reduces greenhouse gases. But most of us never see the massive amounts of water, greenhouse gas emissions, and land used to produce our food. Turns out that the way that we eat is one of the biggest causes of our most pressing environmental issues, from global warming to deforestation; over a quarter of global greenhouse gas emissions come from food. Making small, incremental changes to our diets can drastically improve this. We use computer vision and natural language processing to recognize food options and help you understand where to make those changes.

How it Works

Food Recognition

We automatically recognize the food in an image using artificial intelligence.


Globally Accurate Data

We calculate a sustainability score based on state-of-the-art data from scientific journals.


Menu Recommendation

We give suggestions on the most sustainable item to order on a menu, whether at a restaurant or in a dining hall.
