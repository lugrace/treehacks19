ABOUT EUPHORIA

We know that applying to college is hard. Most college rankings focus on academics and campus life, but few address the mental wellness of the students. EUPHORIA will analyze the happiness of students on campus, helping you decide if it’s the right place for you. It's simple to use. Enter your college and we'll return a number. The higher the number, the happier the college.

How it Works

By analyzing tweets from the student body of a college campus, Euphoria uses natural language processing to assess the mental well-being of the community.

Twitter

What better source to hear the voice students part of the college community than through Twitter? We use the most recent 5,000 tweets (real-time) that contain a message regarding the college/university. We process this data using natural language processing (NLP) to find keywords in the message and to classify the message using sentiment analysis.

Natural Language Processing

We used Microsoft Azure’s Text Analytics API to implement the artificial intelligence aspect of our product, while also performing a sentiment analysis on our tweets. This generates a number, which ranks the tweet on a “good”/”bad” spectrum. By aggregating this number for thousands of tweets, we were able to compute a numerical average, which is the “happiness” factor. This is the value displayed under each college.
